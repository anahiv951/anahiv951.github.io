What makes a hero?

A hero is someone who is recognized for their positive ontributions to society.

Dolores Huerta has contributed to society is various ways such as workers' rights, civil rights, and social justice in the United States. Dolores played a role in improving the working conditions of farmworkers through the Grape Boycott. Dolores also fought against the discrimination of gender dicrimination through the labor movement. Dolores continues to educate both young kids and women through her foundation and speaking engagements.
